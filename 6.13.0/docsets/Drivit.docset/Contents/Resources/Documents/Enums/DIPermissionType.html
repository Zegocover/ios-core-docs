DIPermissionType

public enum DIPermissionType : Int
extension DIPermissionType: CustomStringConvertible

Enum that represents settings identifiers

  • location

    Location identifier

    If you have granted authorization to use your location only when your app is visible, it may compromise the trip recording as it blocks the app from receiving location updates while in the background.

    Do not forget to check if your location precision is on to not affect your trip recording

    Declaration

    Swift

    case location = 100
