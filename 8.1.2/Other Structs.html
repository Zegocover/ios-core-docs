  • Estimates

    Estimates is a computed structure that holds information regarding the future trips estimates based on historical data and a period of time.

    In order to retrieve future trips estimates information the consumer app can use the Reports public interface as follows:

        /// Get the user's future trips estiamates data in a period of 20 days
    DrivitCore.user.reports.estimates(period: 20)
